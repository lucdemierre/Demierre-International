About Me

Hi, I’m Luc Demierre. I’ve always been passionate about cars, watches, and planes—basically anything fast, luxurious, and well-made. From a young age, I’ve been drawn to the world of luxury and speed, and that passion has only grown stronger over time. I love photography, especially when it comes to cars. Car spotting is one of my favorite things to do—finding rare and unique cars on the streets or at events and capturing them through my camera. It’s exciting to catch those moments and preserve them in photos..
